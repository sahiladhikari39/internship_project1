About Nepali Church Boston

Nepali Church Boston is a welcoming faith community located at 561 Main Street,
Melrose, MA 02176, USA. We serve both the Nepali-speaking and English-speaking
communities, creating a spiritual home where cultures meet and faith grows
together.

What makes our church special is the heart of our fellowship—people from
different backgrounds coming together as one family in Christ. Whether you are
most comfortable in Nepali or in English, our services are designed to help you
connect deeply with God, experience genuine community, and live out your faith
in everyday life.

We are more than a place of worship—we are a home where friendships are formed,
prayers are lifted, and lives are transformed through the love of Jesus.

Our Founder – Pastor Subash Luhagun

Nepali Church Boston was founded by Pastor Subash Luhagun, a faithful
servant of God with a deep passion for sharing the Gospel and building a
Christ-centered community. His vision was to create a spiritual home where both
Nepali and English speakers could worship together, grow in faith, and
experience God’s love in unity.

Pastor Subash is known for his dedication, humility, and heart for people.
Through prayer, preaching, and personal care, he has guided countless individuals
in their spiritual journeys. His commitment is not only to lead worship but also
to nurture relationships, encourage the next generation, and serve the community
with compassion.

Under his leadership, Nepali Church Boston has grown into a vibrant and welcoming
congregation—one that reflects his lifelong devotion to Christ and his desire to
see lives transformed through the power of the Gospel.
