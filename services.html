Special Service By Church

Baptism and dedication are sacred milestones in the Christian journey. At Nepali Church
Boston, we joyfully welcome individuals who wish to publicly declare their faith through,
following the example of Jesus. For families, child dedication offers a meaningful
opportunity to commit their children to God’s guidance and blessing, while also seeking
the support of the church community. These ceremonies are held monthly and include prayer,
Scripture, and a warm atmosphere of worship. We encourage participants to prepare through
prayer and reflection, and our pastors are available for guidance and questions prior to
the service.

Marriage Counseling & Weddings

Marriage is a covenant before God, and we are honored to walk alongside couples
preparing for this important step. Our marriage counseling sessions provide
biblical guidance, practical wisdom, and prayerful encouragement to help couples
build a strong foundation for their life together. When it comes to the wedding
ceremony itself, our church offers a Christ-centered service that is personal,
joyful, and tailored to reflect each couple’s unique journey of faith. Whether
you desire a simple gathering or a larger celebration, our pastors and ministry
team will ensure that your wedding is filled with blessing, dignity, and
spiritual depth.

Funeral & Memorial Services

In times of grief and loss, Nepali Church Boston offers compassionate support to individuals
and families. Our funeral and memorial services are designed to honor the life of loved ones
with dignity while providing comfort through the hope found in Christ. Each service includes
prayer, Scripture, and words of encouragement that celebrate a life lived and point toward the
eternal promise of God. We walk closely with families to plan a meaningful service that
reflects their wishes, cultural traditions, and faith values. Our goal is to bring peace,
healing, and spiritual reassurance during one of life’s most difficult seasons.
